Label Literacy for UAE Shoppers

Essential checkpoints

  • Verify bilingual (Arabic/English) labels per UAE.S GSO 9:2015.
  • Check production, expiry, and packed-on dates with Hijri/Gregorian alignment where provided.
  • Look for Halal certification stamp or slaughterhouse ID on meat and poultry.
  • Confirm cold-chain instructions (keep at <=4 degC) on seafood, eggs, and dairy cases.
  • Review country-of-origin, catch area, and farm registration number when printed.

Quick origin decoder

Dubai Municipality carcass seal colours (2023 guidance):

  • Green band - UAE farms.
  • Yellow band - Gulf Cooperation Council suppliers.
  • Blue band - Other Arab countries.
  • Red band - Imported from non-Arab markets.

Ensure seal number matches invoice; reject torn or absent seals.

Frozen Food Handling Snapshot

Why frozen works

Nutrients & benefits

  • Seafood frozen at sea and blanched vegetables retain omega-3 and vitamin C.
  • Helps stabilise pricing by stocking seasonal produce year-round.

Best use

  • Cook vegetables and thin fish straight from frozen to maintain texture.
  • Vacuum seal meats within 48 hours to minimise drip loss.

Risks & watch-outs

  • Heavy frost signals thaw-refreeze cycles-rotate stock promptly.
  • Record product, origin, and freeze date for traceability.

Freezer rules

Core controls

  • Keep freezers at or below -18 degC with weekly logged checks.
  • Store raw items below ready-to-eat frozen foods.

Risks & watch-outs

  • Thaw only under 4 degC refrigeration or cold running water.
  • Never refreeze food warmed above 5 degC.
